# Social Media Do's and Don'ts :-

## Do's 
*  Do enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all your social accounts to add an extra layer of security.
  
*  Do keep your profiles professional and minimal, as they are often checked by recruiters or academic committees.
  
*  Do use strict privacy settings to control who can see your personal information and posts.
  
*  Do verify the authenticity of news or links before sharing them to prevent the spread of misinformation.
  
*  Do respect the digital boundaries of others by asking for permission before tagging them in photos or posts. 

## Don'ts
*  Don't share sensitive personal data like your home address, phone number, or live location publicly.
  
*  Don't post offensive, disrespectful, or controversial content that could damage your reputation as a Student Digital Ambassador.
  
*  Don't share your financial details, UPI PIN, or OTPs with anyone online.
  
*  Don't click on suspicious links or download attachments from unknown senders in your Direct Messages.
  
*  Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying, as responsible communication is a key part of your role.
